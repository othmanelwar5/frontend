# Scientific Proof And Authority

## Goal

Build authority without making illegal or unsafe medical claims. `ميزاني` should feel credible because it explains ingredients clearly, sets honest expectations, and tells customers when to consult a doctor.

## Claim Ladder

Use this hierarchy:

1. Ingredient role: what the ingredient is commonly known for.
2. Routine mechanism: how the product fits daily life.
3. Customer outcome language: comfort, ease, consistency, feeling supported.
4. Safety boundary: not a medicine, results vary, consult doctor when needed.

Avoid turning ingredient education into disease treatment claims.

## Product Proof Angles

### D3/K2 Gummies

Safe authority:

- Vitamin D contributes to normal bone and immune system function.
- Vitamin K contributes to normal blood clotting and is commonly discussed with bone-support routines.
- D3/K2 combinations are common in wellness supplementation, but dosage, deficiency, and medical use require professional advice.

Allowed copy:

`فيتامين D3 معروف بدوره في دعم صحة العظام والمناعة، وK2 يدخل كثيرا في تركيبات دعم العظام. المنتج مصمم كروتين يومي مريح ولا يغني عن التحاليل أو استشارة الطبيب.`

Avoid:

- `يعالج نقص فيتامين د`
- `يحمي من هشاشة العظام`
- `بديل عن وصفة الطبيب`

### Ashwagandha Magnesium Tea

Safe authority:

- Magnesium supports normal nervous system and muscle function.
- Ashwagandha is an adaptogenic herb used in stress-support wellness routines.
- Warm tea can support a consistent wind-down ritual before sleep.

Allowed copy:

`مزيج المغنيسيوم والأشواغاندا يساعدك تبنين طقسا مسائيا أهدأ، بدون وعد بنوم فوري أو علاج للأرق.`

Avoid:

- `يعالج الأرق`
- `ينومك خلال دقائق`
- `بديل لأدوية النوم`

### Probiotic Fiber Gummies

Safe authority:

- Probiotics support beneficial bacteria balance.
- Fiber supports digestive regularity and can help daily digestive comfort.
- Digestive response varies by person and diet.

Allowed copy:

`البروبيوتيك والألياف من المكونات المعروفة في روتين دعم الهضم. ميزاني يقدمها بشكل حلوى يومية سهلة الالتزام.`

Avoid:

- `يعالج عسر الهضم`
- `يقضي على الانتفاخ`
- `حل مضمون لمشاكل القولون`

## Authority UI Blocks

Include these sections on product pages:

- `مكونات معروفة، شرح واضح`
- `طريقة استخدام بسيطة`
- `متى تستشيرين الطبيب؟`
- `منتج عناية يومية وليس دواء`
- `أسئلة شائعة قبل الطلب`

## Citation Handling

If the coder adds citations, use official or high-quality sources such as:

- NIH Office of Dietary Supplements.
- EFSA health claim summaries where relevant.
- Peer-reviewed review articles for ingredient background.
- Brand-owned ingredient education pages only for general education, not as proof of medical outcomes.

Do not show dense academic citations in the main conversion flow. Put reference links in FAQ or an expandable `مراجع تثقيفية` block.

## Certification And Trust Placeholders

Until real certificates exist, use honest placeholders:

- `معلومات المكونات موضحة على العبوة`
- `عبوات مختومة`
- `تأكيد الطلب قبل الشحن`
- `دعم واتساب`

Do not claim:

- SFDA registration.
- GMP certification.
- Lab tested.
- Doctor recommended.

Only add these claims after the user provides real documents or registration numbers.
